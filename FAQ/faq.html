Frequently asked questions

How long does my free trial last?

A Boost free trial is based on how much you use the Editor, not how long you've had an account. You get 20 free actions when you sign up. Analyzing a piece of content and selected a target emotion both use one credit. With 20 credits you can Boost about three pieces of content.

What happens when my free trial ends?

Once you’ve used up all of your trial credits, you can add your credit card on the account page to keep Boosting content.

How does a subscription work?

Boost members are billed every 30 days after upgrading their account. If a member cancels a subscription before the end of a 30 day period, your account will be automatically billed for that month and you will no longer have access to upgraded features. You can cancel at any time if you do not wish to be billed any longer.

Do you offer a discount?

Glad you asked - yes! We offer a 50% discount for our data sharing partners. If you’re interested in joining our data sharing program, you can email us at team@boost-ling.com to get started. Talk to you soon!

What payment methods does Boost accept?

We accept all major credit cards. We do not accept PayPal or any crypto currencies (check back soon). All credit card information is secured on a 3rd party provider that encrypts all information and numbers.

Do you offer customer support?

Of course! When you sign up for a Boost account, a member of our customer succes team will be in touch to make sure you have everything you need to create incredible content .

What information does Boost collect?

For a full in-depth explanation of privacy, please review our Privacy Policy.

What if I want to purchase multiple seats?

Want to share the Boost with your team? Awesome! Each account will need to have a credit card attached to it for the subscription to begin. The same credit card can be entered on multiple different accounts, there are no overlap restrictions.
